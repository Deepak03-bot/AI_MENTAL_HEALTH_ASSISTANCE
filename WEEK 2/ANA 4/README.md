ANA-4 : NHS Benchmark Comparison

This analysis compares CSEW self-reported prevalence rates with NHS mental health referral benchmark rates.

The objective of this analysis is to identify the gap between prevalence indicators and healthcare referral engagement levels.

Key Findings
NHS referral benchmark rates were consistently higher than CSEW self-reported prevalence rates.
A measurable gap existed between prevalence indicators and healthcare referral engagement levels.
NHS referral benchmark rates showed gradual growth across multiple years.
The analysis highlighted the importance of improving mental health support accessibility and healthcare response systems.
